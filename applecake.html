Apple Cake

Description

Apple cake is a type of cake that is made with apples and typically flavored with cinnamon and nutmeg.

  • Apples
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Butter
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Peel and slice apples
  3. Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl
  4. Add butter and mix until crumbly
  5. Add apples and mix well
  6. Pour into greased cake pan
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown
