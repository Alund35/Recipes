Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta that is made with wide, flat noodles and typically layered with meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Lasagna
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  1. Boil water
  2. Add salt and olive oil
  3. Add lasagna noodles
  4. Cook for 8-10 minutes
  5. Drain and rinse with cold water
